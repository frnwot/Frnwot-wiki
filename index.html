    Getting started

    Before going forward, make sure you have met these requirements:

    • Ensure your PC has the latest ADB & Fastboot tools installed with fastboot drivers.
    • Your device must have an unlocked bootloader.
    • Create a full backup of **everything** on your device.
    • OTA updates are supported.
    • Custom kernels are **NOT SUPPORTED**, do **NOT** flash them.
    • Flash LineageOS **ONLY** using Lineage Recovery (installation guide included).
    • I apply patches to the vanilla LineageOS source—find them here.
    • Most device sources used in my builds are in my repo here.
